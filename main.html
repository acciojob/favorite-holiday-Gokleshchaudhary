Paris


Paris, the capital of France, is known for its art, fashion, and culture. It is home to iconic landmarks such as the Eiffel Tower and the Louvre Museum.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower - A global cultural icon of France.
  2. Louvre Museum - The world’s largest art museum.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral - A masterpiece of French Gothic architecture.
  4. Champs-Élysées - Famous avenue known for its theaters and cafés.
  5. Montmartre - A historic district known for its artistic history.

Must-Try Foods

  • Baguette - A long, narrow loaf of French bread.
  • Macarons - Colorful meringue-based confections.
  • Coq au Vin - A classic French dish of chicken braised with wine.
  • Ratatouille - A vegetable dish that originated in Provence.
  • Crêpes - Thin pancakes that can be sweet or savory.
