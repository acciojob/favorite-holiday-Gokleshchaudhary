Paris


Paris, the capital of France, is renowned for its art, fashion, and culture. It's known for its iconic landmarks like the Eiffel Tower and the Louvre Museum, making it a must-visit destination for travelers.

  1. Eiffel Tower - A global cultural icon of France.
  2. Louvre Museum - The world's largest art museum and a historic monument.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral - A masterpiece of French Gothic architecture.
  4. Montmartre - A historic district known for its bohemian past.
  5. Champs-Élysées - Famous for its theaters, cafés, and luxury shops.
  • Croissant - A buttery, flaky pastry perfect for breakfast.
  • Escargot - A delicacy made from cooked land snails.
  • Macarons - Colorful meringue-based cookies filled with ganache.
  • Ratatouille - A traditional Provençal vegetable dish.
  • Crêpes - Thin pancakes that can be sweet or savory.
